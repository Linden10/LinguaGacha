Then use JSONLINE to output review results, without extra explanation or clarification:
```jsonline
{"src":"<Source Term>","verdict":"<VERDICT>","suggested_dst":"<Corrected Translation or empty if KEEP>","reason":"<Brief reason>"}
```
Where VERDICT is one of: KEEP, FIX, REMOVE
- KEEP: Entry is accurate, no changes needed. Leave "suggested_dst" empty.
- FIX: Entry has issues but can be corrected. Provide the corrected translation in "suggested_dst".
- REMOVE: Entry should be removed. Explain why in "reason".
